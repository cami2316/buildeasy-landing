See real examples

Everything that usually gets lost in WhatsApp messages, notes and photos becomes organized inside BuildEasy.

1. Simple message → Professional estimate

BEFORE (WhatsApp message):

“tile 250 feet, 600 dollar, you buy material. start Wednesday if ok.”
AFTER (inside BuildEasy):

  • Service: Demo + tile installation – 250 sq ft
  • Prep: Basic leveling included
  • Materials: Provided by the client
  • Suggested price: $1,150 – $1,350
  • Payment terms: 50% upfront • 50% on completion
  • Ready-to-send text for email or WhatsApp

2. Wrong square footage → Automatic correction

BEFORE:

“Kitchen is 12x15, so about 120 feet, right?”
AFTER (in the app):

“12 x 15 ft = 180 sq ft. The original calculation was off by 60 sq ft. Suggestion: update the price to avoid losing money.”

3. Weak invoice → Invoice that inspires trust

BEFORE:

Invoice with one line only, no description, no terms, no deadline.
AFTER:

  • Clear description of work done in each area.
  • Defined execution timeline.
  • Payment methods explained.
  • Service warranty details.
  • Professional, clean, client-friendly text.

4. Receipt photo → Organized expense

BEFORE:

Receipt photo lost in the gallery, with no notes and no expense recorded.
AFTER (inside BuildEasy):

  • Vendor identified (e.g., Home Depot).
  • Date detected.
  • Total amount extracted automatically.
  • Category: material, labor, transport…
  • Linked to the correct job instantly.
